About GrillBox

GrillBox is a newly opened small Korean restaurant in the Quezon City. It officially took off and was readily available for orders of customers on May 6, 2023. The owners decided to open up this Korean restaurant to bring Filipinos more korean cuisines at the comfort of their homes around Metro Manila.

GrillBox is the best source of korean
food cravings in the Philippines.
Savor the taste of authentic Korean
flavors inside GrillBox at the comfort
of your homes!

By providing our customers with a Korean restaurant experience without them having to leave their homes, our company hopes to serve as a catalyst for the Korean food industry. As a result, we deliver more than just food to our customers; we also deliver the best service and the best equipment, giving them the full experience of a genuine Korean restaurant right in their own homes.
